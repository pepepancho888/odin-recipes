Pique Macho

Description

This is a yummy recipe from Bolivia. It is very simple to prepare but it tastes amazing. My mother always prepares this dish for me on my birthday.

Ingredients

  • 1 Kilo of soft meat
  • ½ kilo of sausages
  • 10 medium potatoes
  • 4 large onions
  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • 3 locotos or chili peppers
  • oil
  • ground pepper
  • ground cumin
  • minced garlic
  • salt

Steps

  1. Cut the meat into small pieces and season with salt, garlic, pepper and cumin to taste.
  2. Chop the locoto or chili peppers into strips and sauté in the pan with a little hot oil, with the finely chopped onion, the diced tomato and a pinch of salt.
  3. In another pan, fry the meat and the sliced sausages, cover the pan to obtain a juicy meat.
  4. Cut the potatoes into strips and fry them in oil.
  5. Mix everything together and add the fried potatoes.
  6. Serve garnished with pieces of chopped raw locoto, onions and sliced tomatoes.
  7. To make it a pique criollo you can add cheese slices and sliced hard-boiled eggs.
