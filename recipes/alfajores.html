Alfajores

Description

This is a delicious recipe that comes from Argentina. It's made with two soft cookies forming a sandwich with dulce de leche in the middle and everything is coated in chocolate. It's one of my favorite foods in the world.

Ingredients

    COATING

  • Semi-sweet chocolate 300 grams
  • Cocoa butter 40 grams

    • DOUGH

  • Butter 200 grams
  • Honey 50 grams
  • Eggs 2
  • Flour 400 grams
  • Lemon zest 1
  • Water 50 cc
  • Corn Starch 100 grams
  • Sugar 200 grams
  • Baking soda 1 pinch
  • Bitter cocoa 20 grams

    • FILLING

  • Dulce de leche 500 grams

Steps

    DOUGH

  1. Beat the softened butter with the sugar until white.
  2. Add the lemon zest, honey and eggs.
  3. Sift the flour, cocoa and cornstarch and add to the mixture.
  4. Dissolve the baking soda in the water, add and mix without kneading much.
  5. Wrap in plastic wrap and chill in the refrigerator.
  6. Stretch the dough 3 mm and cut discs 5 cm in diameter.
  7. Place them on a plate lightly greased with butter.
  8. Bake at 180 ° C for 10 minutes, cool and reserve.

    9. ASSEMBLY

  9. Fill two discs with dulce de leche and join them together.
  10. Melt the previously grated cocoa butter in a bain-marie.
  11. Add the chopped and melted chocolate when melted.
  12. Coat the alfajores with the chocolate at 40°C and let the chocolate drain.
  13. Let stand 48 hours before consuming.
