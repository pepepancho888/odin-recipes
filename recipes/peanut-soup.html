Peanut Soup

Description

This is an amazing soup from Bolivia. It is served with a spicy sauce called llajua. You can find this food in all the country.

Ingredients

  • ½ Kg beef, better if it has some bone
  • 1 cup raw peeled peanuts
  • 1 white onion peeled and chopped
  • 1 carrot cut into cubes
  • ½ cup of peeled peas
  • 1 diced celery stalk
  • ½ diced bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 ½ liters beef or vegetable broth
  • 4 potatoes cut into large squares
  • salt
  • pepper
  • cumin
  • oregano
  • half bag of large noodles
  • parsley chopped
  • llajwa or spicy sauce

Steps

  1. In a small pan with 1 tablespoon of oil, fry the peeled peanuts
  2. Once the peanuts are golden, grind or blend them with a little water.
  3. In a large pot, add 6 cups of broth and the ground peanuts.
  4. Add the beef and let it boil.
  5. In a small frying pan with 3 tablespoons of oil, fry the chopped onion until slightly brown, add the pepper, oregano, cumin and salt.
  6. Mix a little and fry for about 2 more minutes and add it to the soup.
  7. With a wooden spoon, mix and cook for 1 hour.
  8. Add the noodles, the peas and the potatoes.
  9. Let boil 20 minutes or until potatoes and peas are cooked.
  10. Remove from heat and serve sprinkling chopped parsley on top.
